<objective>
Propagate context.Context parameters through 4 files where context.Background() was used instead of accepting a context from callers.

Purpose: Enable proper request-scoped cancellation, deadline propagation, and tracing. Using context.Background() in request-scoped operations breaks distributed tracing and prevents graceful cancellation.
Output: 4 modified files with ctx parameters propagated through method signatures and call chains.
</objective>
